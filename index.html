Protection Orders

A Protection Order is a type of "restraining order" that you, (the petitioner), can file against another person, (the respondent), if you believe you have been a victim of domestic violence by the other person.

Criminal Division

The Criminal Division is the largest of four divisions in the King County Prosecutor's Office and is organized into several different units and sections. The Criminal Division represents the state and the county in criminal matters in the King County District and Superior courts, the state and federal courts of appeal, and the Washington and U.S. Supreme Courts.

LEARN MORE

Civil Division

The Civil Division provides comprehensive legal services to King County government, including all County elected and appointed officials, department heads, and with the managers of numerous county agencies and departments.
